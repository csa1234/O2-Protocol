How it works

1) O2 Protocol finance CO2 projects certification at verra.org, binded with a legal agreement with project owner
2) Verra certification is tokenized thru Toucan Protocol NFT
3) NFT is converted to Toucan Protocol BCT token and swaped to USDT
4) O2 Protocol keeps 50% of the USDT and 50% goes to project owner
5) 50% of the USDT is swaped to buy back O2P token and remaining will be used
to finance new projects certifcation and provide liquidity to the pools

BUSINESS MODEL O2P TOKEN

Finance of carbon offset projects certification with 50% share of the certification value based on the blockchain price.

FINANCE

Toucan protocol bridge and monetization of carbon offset projects.

MONETIZATION

Receive a share on O2 Protocol revenues.
Carbon credit project owner will have access to exclusive and special financial benefits.

SMART MANAGEMENT

RETIRE

TOKENIZATION

Liquidity will become available from the profits generated after the monetization of each financed carbon offset project, creating a constant money flow income to the platform.

LEVERAGED FARMING

O2 Protocol will constantly create income from real world financial assets based on carbon offset certfications and buying back O2P tokens from the market creating buy pressure.

About Us

O2 Protocol started as a concept idea in early 2019.
By 2020 O2 Protocol started a pre-incubation process with the National University
of Asuncion, Start-up Lab division and we finished in early 2022 after a long research
in several areas like marketing, finance, carbon offset market, agricultural business
among others area.

By mid 2022 we started the final incubation process and the final result stage is now.

This is the first time in all crypto world, were a DeFi project combines with
a real business model like carbon offset trading and regenerate money flow to
the crypto enviroment.
